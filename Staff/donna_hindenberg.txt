Donna Hindenberg, BA, MBA, CIM
Investment Advisor
Donna Hindenberg is an investment advisor at Rundle Investments, which she joined in 2017. Prior to joining Mawer, she worked as a wealth management advisor at Sailor Moon Capital Management.
Mrs. Hindenberg earned her Bachelor of Arts in Communications from Universidade Estadual Paulista in Brazil (2002) and an MBA from Vancouver Island University in Nanaimo, BC (2011). She is also a Chartered Investment Manager and speaks both Portuguese and Spanish.
